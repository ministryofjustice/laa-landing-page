Delegate access

This form allows you to invite an external user to log into the LAA Portal and view your firm information. Depending on the permissions you set, it will allow them to access and perform actions in Portal applications such as CWA & CCMS.

Continue this process if you want to invite:

  • A costs draftsperson
  • A barrister or barristers Clerk
! Warning Not for Firm Staff

This is for inviting guests only. If you are looking to create an account for member of your provider form, who does not need access to multiple provider firms, instead used the 'Create user' link found on the User Management screen.
